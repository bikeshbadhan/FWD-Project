Empower Your Future with SIT _

Summer Institution Training (SIT) at IKGPTU is a mandatory program for all enrolled students, designed to enhance your academic and professional skills. This exclusive training ensures you are well-prepared for the competitive job market with cutting-edge courses tailored to IKGPTU’s curriculum.

Program Overview

SIT offers specialized modules in Web Design, Programming, and Microsoft Office, mandatory for all IKGPTU students. These courses are integrated into your academic schedule, providing hands-on experience with the latest tools and technologies relevant to your degree.

Key Benefits

  • Strengthen your resume with certified skills
  • Access to IKGPTU’s expert faculty and resources
  • Seamless integration with your academic progress
  • Exclusive opportunities for campus placements

Next Steps

All IKGPTU students are automatically enrolled in SIT. Check your schedule on the university portal and attend the orientation session scheduled for July 15, 2025, to get started.
